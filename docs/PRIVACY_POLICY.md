# Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026

JobHuntHQ ("we", "our", "us") provides a job tracking application that helps users organize applications, resumes, companies, and job-related email imports. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Account and profile data
- Email address
- Name (if provided by your identity provider)
- Profile image (if provided by your identity provider)

### B. Application workspace data
- Job applications (company, role, status, notes, source, dates)
- Resume records and links you provide
- Company records derived from your usage
- Imported email metadata used for review workflows

### C. Authentication and integration data
- OAuth identifiers needed for sign-in
- Google OAuth access data required to enable Gmail integration

### D. Technical and operational data
- Basic request and error logs required to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the service
- Security telemetry such as rate-limit and request validation outcomes

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:
- Authenticate users and secure access to accounts
- Provide core features (tracking applications, resumes, companies, and review queues)
- Import and process job-related Gmail messages when you initiate sync
- Maintain platform security, reliability, and abuse prevention
- Diagnose incidents and improve product quality

## 3. Gmail and Google API Data

If you connect Google, JobHuntHQ requests access needed for sign-in and Gmail review features.

- Gmail access is used only to retrieve and process job-related emails for your in-app review workflow.
- We do not sell Google user data.
- We do not use Gmail data for advertising.
- We do not transfer Google user data to third parties except as necessary to operate the service (for example, infrastructure providers under contract), comply with law, or protect rights and safety.

Use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including Limited Use requirements.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We may share data only:
- With service providers who process data on our behalf (hosting, database, monitoring)
- To comply with legal obligations, enforce terms, or respond to lawful requests
- To protect the security, rights, and safety of users and the service

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active or as needed to provide the service. We may retain limited records for security, legal, or compliance reasons where required.

## 6. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information, including authenticated access controls, ownership scoping, transport security, and abuse protections. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

## 7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account data
- Object to or restrict certain processing
- Request data portability where applicable

To exercise rights, contact us using the details below.

## 8. International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border data transfers.

## 9. Children's Privacy

JobHuntHQ is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a revised effective date.
